# The Puno Calculus — v1.0.0

**July 2026**

The derivative folds. The integral unfolds. The crease is where the action is.

## What this is

A unified framework connecting neural network theory, calculus, special
relativity, thermodynamics (Landau theory, critical phenomena), chemistry,
nuclear physics, protein folding, and quantum cosmology through a single
geometric idea: that folding and unfolding are the fundamental operations
of physical systems, and the crease — the boundary between them — is
where all the interesting behavior happens.

## Repository structure

The repo is organized into layers that trace the idea's development:

- **0-ROOTS/** — Five verified experiments on crease density in ReLU networks
  (code + results + the full Book of Puno monograph)
- **1-GEOMETRY/** — The 90-degree fold as Minkowski spacetime, plus the cusp
  catastrophe appendix
- **2-CREASE-AS-GENERATOR/** — The "sheet has teeth" mechanism: water,
  nuclear fusion/fission, protein folding
- **3-IMAGINARY-SPREAD/** — Signature change, Hawking-Hartle no-boundary,
  honest critique of the problems
- **4-PHASE-AS-CREASE/** — Phase transitions through the fold lens
  (sulcification, laser threshold, water's density anomaly)
- **9-TOWARDS/** — The unified Crease Principle, open questions, and the
  formal Landau theory bridge (CUSP_TO_CRITICAL.md)

## Verified

Layer 0 contains five independently reproducible experiments on ReLU
neural networks, verified to three or four decimal places. All other
layers are theoretical development.
